Common Liver & Surgery Questions

Conditions include benign biliary stricture, gall bladder cancer, bile duct cancer, and choledochal cysts. Treatments vary depending on the specific condition and its severity.

Symptoms of liver tumors may include abdominal pain and jaundice. Treatments involve surgery, medication, or targeted therapies, depending on the tumor type and stage.

Acute pancreatitis may require hospitalization for complications like pseudocysts. Pancreatic tumors, including neuroendocrine tumors and cystic neoplasms, are treated with surgery, chemotherapy, or targeted therapies.

Liver transplantation involves replacing a diseased liver with a donor liver. Success rates are high, with one-year survival around 80-90% and five-year survival about 70-80%.
