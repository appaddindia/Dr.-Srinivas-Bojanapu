Liver Transplant Surgery

Dr. Srinivas Bojanapu has performed over 2500+ liver transplantation surgeries considered to be best liver transplant surgeon in bangalore. Liver Transplant Surgery or hepatic transplantation is the replacement of a diseased liver with some or all of a healthy liver from another person. The most commonly used technique is orthotopic transplantation A few facts to be known about the liver transplant:

  • Liver Transplantation is needed only as a cure of the advanced cirrhosis stages .
  • It should be done only by the close relative or by the donation of person of brain dead and should match the blood group.
  • When it is done in time, its success rate would be 80%.
  • Once the transplant is done, close observation and follow up are to be done essentially.
  • Once the transplant is successful, the person can lead completely normal life.
